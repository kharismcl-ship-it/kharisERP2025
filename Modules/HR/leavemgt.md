- **Maternity/Paternity Leave** - Statutory compliance
- **Emergency Leave** - Quick approval processes
- **Unpaid Leave** - Separate tracking
- **Special Leaves** - Bereavement, jury duty, etc.

**2. Advanced Workflow Features:**

- **Delegation rules** for absent approvers

- **Auto-approval** for certain leave types
- **Emergency approval** bypass for urgent cases

**3. Accrual & Balance Management:**
- **Pro-rata accrual** calculations
- **Carry-forward rules** with expiration
- **Leave year configuration** (calendar vs fiscal)
- **Balance adjustments** with audit trails
- **Encashment policies**

**4. Employee Self-Service:**
- **Leave calendar** with team visibility
- **Balance statements** and history
- **Leave forecast** and planning tools
- **Mobile access** for requests on-the-go

**5. Compliance & Reporting:**
- **Statutory compliance** tracking
- **Leave utilization reports**
- **Trend analysis** and forecasting
- **Audit trails** for all changes
